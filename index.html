WELCOME TO NIELIT COLLEGE OF EDUCATION OFFICIAL WEBSITE


NIELIT Imphal was established under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, in 1994. As a premier institution in the North Eastern region, NIELIT Imphal has been working tirelessly for over two decades to provide high-quality technical education and digital literacy to the youth of Manipur and beyond. Starting with a small and committed team of professionals, the institute, located at Akampat, Imphal East, has grown into a leading hub for IT education and skilling. It offers a wide range of programs including BCA, MCA, and various short-term and long-term industry-oriented courses. Vision... the ultimate goal of technical education should be to empower students with the skills to shape their future and uplift society. Inspired by this belief, NIELIT Imphal focuses on producing not just skilled professionals but responsible digital citizens. The institute strives to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and innovation among its students. It aims to prepare learners for a rapidly changing technological world while fostering a sense of responsibility and purpose. Through a holistic learning environment, NIELIT Imphal encourages students to discover their potential, stay resilient, and become agents of positive change. Our guiding values—Discipline, Dedication, and Development—are the core principles that shape every aspect of our work.

ACCREDITATION

National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bangalore accredited and awarded B+ grade on 10th February 2007. It is the first college in Manipur which is ever assessed by the NAAC before completion of 4th year of its inauguration. Further, NAAC assessed the College for 2nd Cycle of Accreditation and awarded B Grade on June 25, 2015
